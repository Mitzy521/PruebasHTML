The Colors

How do you know the colors are clasificated?

Primary Colors

These colors are the fundamental building blocks of all other colors. You can't create them by mixing other colors. They're like the original three musketeers of the color wheel.

Secondary colors

These colors are what you get when you mix two primary colors together in equal parts. Think of them as the direct offspring of the primaries.

Tertiary Colors

These colors are created by mixing a primary color with an adjacent secondary color. These colors often have a two-word name, combining the names of the primary and secondary colors used, with the primary color always listed first.
