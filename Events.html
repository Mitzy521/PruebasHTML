Upcoming Events

Community Garden Volunteer Day

Join us for a day of planting and prepping at the Cobán Community Garden. We'll be sowing seeds for the fall harvest, weeding, and sprucing up the pathways. No experience is necessary—just bring your enthusiasm and a pair of gloves! It's a great opportunity to learn about gardening, meet new people, and help make our community a little greener.

Cobán Star Gazing Night

Explore the cosmos with local astronomers at our annual Star Gazing Night. We'll have telescopes set up for you to get an up-close look at planets like Jupiter and Saturn, as well as various constellations and galaxies. A short presentation will be given on the night sky, and experts will be on hand to answer all your questions. This is a perfect event for families and anyone curious about the universe.

Past Events

Past Event 1: Cobán Coffee Festival

On May 10, 2025, the community celebrated the annual Cobán Coffee Festival. Attendees enjoyed tastings of locally grown coffee, learned about the bean-to-cup process from expert farmers, and enjoyed live marimba music. The festival highlighted Cobán's rich coffee heritage and provided a platform for local growers to showcase their high-quality beans.

Past Event 2: Ancient Maya Ruins Tour

On June 20, 2025, a guided tour of the nearby Maya ruins was held. Participants explored the ancient pyramids and temples, learning about the history, culture, and astronomical knowledge of the Maya civilization. The tour was a fascinating journey back in time, providing insight into the region's historical significance and archaeological wonders
